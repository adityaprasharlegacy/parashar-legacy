The Journey Begins

I am Aditya Pratap Dubey, born on July 17, 2005, in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. At 19, I reflect on a life that has already seen doubt, confusion, and transformation. My early school years lacked direction, but a spark changed everything: astrology.

Path of the Stars

After endless calls and research, I was drawn to Rajasthan, where I now study BA in Astrology under the guidance of Dr. Bhavyadeep Bhartiya—recognized in the India Book of Records. This path feels like destiny.

The Parashar Legacy

My lineage traces back to Rishi Parashar, the father of astrology, through Vashishta and Shakti. Knowing this gave meaning to my intuitive talents. I’ve always had a gift—sensing energies, reading people, offering advice that brings peace.
